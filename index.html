About NurseShift

NurseShift is a Sunday shift rotation manager built for nursing teams — designed to keep scheduling fair, transparent, and effortless.

The original inspiration for NurseShift comes from Ersie Alexandre, whose dedication to fair and organized shift coordination made this tool a reality.
